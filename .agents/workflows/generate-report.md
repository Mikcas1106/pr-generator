---
description: How to generate a professional progress report using GitReport PRO
---

1. **Setup Your Projects**
   - Navigate to the **Projects List** tab.
   - Click **+ Add Project**.
   - Fill in your local repository path, supervisor's name, and repo platform details.
   - Ensure the "Include in Report" toggle is active.

2. **Configure Recurring Tasks**
   - Go to the **Defaults & Holidays** tab.
   - Click **+ Add Default Task** for recurring events (e.g., Daily Scrum, Weekly Sync).
   - Link them to specific projects or leave as "(Global)" for general overhead.

3. **Select Your Date Range**
   - Head to the **Report Engine** tab.
   - Use the **Smart Date Shortcuts** (e.g., "1st Week", "2nd Week") for instant date selection, or pick manually using the calendar.
   - Verify your **Git Email Filter** matches your repo's commit history.

4. **Launch Analysis**
   - Click the **Generate Progress Report Now** button.
   - Wait for the cinematic loading animation to complete.

5. **Final Review & Live Edit**
   - In the **Report Preview Modal**, review the auto-detected commits and tasks.
   - Use the **Search Bar** to find specific entries.
   - **Toggle off** any rows you wish to exclude.
   - **Edit remarks** directly in the table if extra details are needed.

6. **Export & Download**
   - Click **Export to Excel**.
   - Your professional `.xlsx` file will automatically download with a standardized name.
