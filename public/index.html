Welcome to Git Report v2

Let's quickly walk through the new automation features to make your progress reports effortlessly across multiple projects.

1. Set Your Identity

Fill in your **Employee Name** and **ID**. Be sure to use your **Git Email Filter** (e.g., your email username) so the tool finds only your work.

2. Add Projects

Paste the **Local Repository Path** of each project you worked on. This builds your master project list for the whole app.

3. Dropdown Linking

Default tasks now use a **Project Dropdown**. Selecting a project automatically fills and locks the **Supervisor** field for perfect consistency.

4. Color-Coded Reports

Categorize tasks as **Meeting (Blue)**, **Database (Gray)**, or **Holiday (Green)**. The generated Excel will be automatically highlighted!

5. Tasks & Remarks

Add recurring tasks with custom **Remarks**. Use the **Include to Report** checkbox to toggle rows on/off without deleting them.

Ready to Go!

Click **Generate Now** to build your professional Excel report. Your settings are autosaved for next time. Ready?
